What is SPACE 101?

We go about our daily lives understanding almost nothing of the world. We give little thought to the machinary that generates the sunlight that makes life possible, to the gravity that glues us to an Earth that would otherwise send us spinning off into Space. Our mission is to provide information, up-to-date news about the world of cosmos!
